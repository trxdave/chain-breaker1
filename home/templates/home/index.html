Why This Matters

Human trafficking is a pervasive and devastating issue that affects millions of people worldwide. Victims are often coerced, deceived, or manipulated into situations of forced labor, sexual exploitation, or domestic servitude. It happens in every country, to people of all ages, and across all walks of life.

At Chain Breakers, we believe that raising awareness and providing accessible, practical tools is essential to combating modern slavery. Together, we can create a world where everyone has the freedom to live without fear of exploitation.

The Reality of Human Trafficking: By the Numbers

Global Statistics

Human trafficking is a global crisis affecting over 50 million people worldwide. The industry generates $150 billion annually, exploiting vulnerable individuals for forced labor and sexual exploitation. These figures highlight the urgent need for global awareness and action.

EU and UK Statistics

In the UK, an estimated 136,000 people live in modern slavery, while the EU reports over 14,000 registered victims annually. These statistics emphasize the significant impact of trafficking within Europe and the importance of localized efforts to combat it.
